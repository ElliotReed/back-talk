Backtalk refers to rude, disrespectful, or argumentative replies,
particularly from children to authority figures like parents or teachers. It often involves a negative tone, body language, or insolent language used to express frustration or challenge power. Common in children and teens, it is generally considered a form of defiance. 

It is an impudent or insolent retort, often described as "sass" or "lip".
